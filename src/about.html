About Me

My name is Adimora Favour Arinze based in Enugu State, Nigeria.

I'm a sophomore Mechatronics Engineering student at Bells University of Technology. My passion for technology led me to begin my coding journey in August 2023.

I started by diving into C++ and 3D modeling with Autodesk Fusion 360, which solidified my interest in the field.

Currently, I'm actively expanding my skillset by learning web development. This includes mastering core languages like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, along with frameworks like Bootstrap and React JS.

I'm driven to apply my newfound knowledge to real-world projects and contribute meaningfully to the ever-changing tech landscape.

My ultimate vision is to merge my expertise in coding with my proficiency in CAD software. I believe this combined skillset will serve as a strong foundation for a future career in robotics and software engineering. I'm committed to leveraging my technical abilities to make a lasting impact in these fields.
